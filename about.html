About Miriam Baer

Miriam offers compassionate, non-judgmental life coaching designed to help you unlock your full potential and build a more connected, fulfilling life.

Miriam combines gentle presence, evidence-informed practices, and practical tools to create a supportive environment where clients can explore patterns, soften judgment, and develop meaningful habits. Her approach is warm, reassuring, and focused on small, sustainable steps.

What to expect

  • Confidential, supportive coaching sessions
  • Tools to identify triggers and shift responses
  • A focus on compassionate self-inquiry and practical change
